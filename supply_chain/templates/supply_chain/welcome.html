Welcome to Solana Supply Chain

This application demonstrates a supply chain management system built on the Solana blockchain and IPFS.

Key Features:

  • Transfer products between organizations
  • Track product history and transactions
  • Store product data and certificates on IPFS
  • Utilize Solana blockchain for secure and fast transactions
