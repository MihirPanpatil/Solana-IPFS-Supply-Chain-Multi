Welcome to BlockHarvest

BlockHarvest is a cutting-edge supply chain management system built on the Solana blockchain and IPFS. Our platform revolutionizes how organizations track, manage, and secure their supply chain operations.

Key Features

Product Management

Add, edit, and manage products with ease. Track every detail throughout the supply chain.

Blockchain Integration

Leverage Solana's fast and secure blockchain for transparent, immutable transaction records.

IPFS Storage

Store product data and certificates on IPFS for decentralized and reliable access.

Transfer Tracking

Seamlessly transfer products between organizations and track the entire journey.

Project Structure

  • Frontend: Django templates with Tailwind CSS for responsive design
  • Backend: Django framework for robust server-side logic
  • Blockchain: Solana for fast and efficient transactions
  • Storage: IPFS for decentralized file storage

Advantages

  1. Enhanced Transparency: Full visibility of product movement and history
  2. Improved Security: Blockchain-backed data integrity and authenticity
  3. Efficient Operations: Streamlined processes for faster supply chain management
  4. Cost Reduction: Minimize intermediaries and reduce operational costs
  5. Real-time Updates: Instant access to the latest supply chain information
